Safe Haven Ministries International Inc.



Psalm 91:4 Est. 2021


Safe Haven is a virtual ministry that holds weekly prayer and bible study and monthly e-gatherings.
Safe Haven also does community outreach and services from America to Africa.

Mission

The purpose of this ministry is to counsel, teach and bless people with the word of God. As well as to be a blessing to people with basic needs such as food and clothing from local to global.

Our Mission is to allow souls the opportunity to find healing at the altar, hope through the word of the Lord, and help out of the sanctuary.

Our Vision is to teach the word of God, train disciples, and tell all the truth of the gospel of Jesus Christ.

History


On January 2nd of 2021 Daniella was called and confirmed to start a prayer gathering. The prayer gathering was to invite all to encounter the power of God to heal, give them hope, to help them recover and allow God to restore them after such a hard year filled with death and disease (the pandemic).


After a few months of prayer on Saturdays the people were asking for more worship and more teaching. People where driving from across the state to be apart of the prayer gatherings and still are.

Then Daniella made the careful decision to answer the call to build the Kingdom of God and expand Safe Haven's borders from local to global by going virtual.

About

Daniella is the founder of Safe Haven Ministries. On July 1st , 2016 Daniella and her daughter moved to Venice, FL from Long Island, NY. Daniella 33 at the time relinquished her position at Friendship Baptist Church Worldwide to answer the call to come too Florida to build the Kingdom of God. Daniella was sent and confirmed by her congregation in NY and reaffirmed by several assemblies here in Florida.

Daniella has been in Florida for 5 years and since she has helped plant and build 3 ministries and 2 churches. She has traveled all over the state to preach the gospel and has held several community services to advance the kingdom, save souls and revolutionize the body of Christ. Products

Atarah

Atarah means crown in Hebrew. The Atarah is a part of the Hebrew Tallit or known to Christians as the Prayer Shawl. The significance of the Atarah on the Tallit is that it is a reminder that we are blessed by God.

According to the Rabbis, the reason the Atarah was put on the Tallit is to beautify the head. The Tallit is worn as a covering on the head and the head is known in the Hebrew culture as the most "esteemed" part of the body.

Many Rabbi's both current and past see the Atarah as an option you can use to beautify the Tallit with it on the collar or not.
For more information and to purchase email us or visit daniellaameho.com/atarah
